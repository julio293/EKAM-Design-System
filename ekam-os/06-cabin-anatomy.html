Aरेखा · the plan

The reference cabin. 6.4 × 3.6 metres.

23 m² floor plate. One room. The bed faces the window. The kitchenette and bathroom are on the back wall. The deck extends beyond the window. Every tier (Kutir / Van / Shikhar) varies this plate by ≤ 15%; the spine of the cabin is invariant.

— Floor area   Interior 23.0 m² · deck 10.2 m² · footprint 33.2 m². The bed faces the window. The kitchenette and bathroom are on the back wall, away from the view. The door enters from the side — never from the front, so the first thing the guest sees is the window, not the entry.
