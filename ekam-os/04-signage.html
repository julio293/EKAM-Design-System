Every sign on site, drawn once.

Six categories. Forty-three panels. One material palette (`03-materials`). One typography ramp (`02-spatial-type`). Every plate, plinth and post in this document is vendor-quotable — dimension, mounting, finish, lifespan are specified. Nothing is decorative.

Aवर्ग · the typology

Six signage categories. One vocabulary across.

Every sign on an EKAM property maps to one of these six. If a sign cannot be placed in a category, it is not built.

Bद्वार · the gate

The gate marker. Stone, carved, permanent.

A single sandstone slab set into the entry path. V-cut letters at 100 mm cap height. No paint fill — the carve carries the legibility. The only sign on the property meant to outlive the property.

Gनिर्माण-नियम · build rules

Build rules. Apply before any vendor quote.

— Rule 01

All outdoor plates: forest powder-coat only.

RAL 6009. Matte 10% gloss. No paint variants. No anodised aluminium that "looks like" forest.

— Rule 02

All lettering: cream vinyl or cream screen-print.

No painted-on-paint. No engraving filled with paint. No laser-cut-through (weakens the plate).

— Rule 03

All mounts: concrete plinth or forest MS post.

No tree-mounting. No stake-into-soil (frost-heaves in winter). No screws into cabin cladding.

— Rule 04

All heights: cap to spatial-type ramp.

Gate 100 mm · site map 28 mm title · cabin plate 48 mm numeral · house-rule 14 mm title · utility 11 mm. See `02-spatial-type`.

— Rule 05

All bindus: scale to plate, not to ramp.

10% of the largest letter on the plate. Never below 5 mm absolute diameter on any signage surface.

— Anti-rule

No backlighting, anywhere.

The cabin window is the only light on the property after dusk. Signage is read by moonlight, lantern, or the next morning.
