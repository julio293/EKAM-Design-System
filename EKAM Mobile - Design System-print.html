— B · Canonical sizes

Three sizes. Nothing in between.

20×17 dp · sm Inline in chips, dense rows, beside a cabin name
40×33 dp · md Card eyebrow row, saved-list left anchor

— C · Use here
  • Property cards — eyebrow row, left of the cabin name.
  • Property detail page — top-left, paired with the cabin name.
  • Saved-cabin list — left-anchored on each row, identifies tier at a glance.
  • Booking confirmation summary — above the cabin name.
  • Tier filter chips — when the user is filtering by Kutir / Van / Shikhar.
  • Tier-overview surface — the Network chapter's "what each tier is" map.
— D · Not here
  • Ornament on a non-tier screen — Settings, Profile, Onboarding splash.
  • Multiple instances on a single screen, unless intentionally comparing tiers.
  • List markers or eyebrow decoration — they are not bullets.
  • Inside body copy mid-sentence — words carry the meaning.
  • Animated, rotated, pulsed in any way — the glyph holds still.
  • Restyled — never recoloured beyond forest-on-light and cream-on-forest. The clay detail is intentional, not optional.
02दृश्य · the view

Photography that breathes.

One photograph per surface, sized to the eye and given room. Cabin and land first — never people in foreground. The frame leaves room for the title to land without burying it.

— Reference shot · the brand register

A lit room inside a hush. The frame is the cabin, the field, and the weather — in that order.

Mood · Quiet · Patient
Time · Dusk · 16:9
Used · Hero · OG · Print
✓ Always
  • The cabin within the landForest, ridgeline, weather. The architecture is small inside the frame, never the whole picture.
  • Natural light, real weatherDusk and dawn first. Mist, drizzle, falling snow — read as time of day, not retouching.
  • One warm windowIf the cabin is lit, only one or two windows glow. Restraint reads as occupied.
  • Negative space left for typeThe bottom-left quarter stays quiet so headlines can land.
✕ Never
  • People in the foregroundCouples on rocks, lifestyle moments. The brand is what they are about to walk into, not what they are doing.
  • Saturated sunsets, lens flaresAggressive saturation, orange-and-teal grading. Our palette is muted on purpose.
  • Drone overheads of empty landTravel-magazine cliché. Eye-level or a single elevated profile only.
  • Brand wordmark inside the photoThe wordmark lives outside the image, on the cream. The photo is not a billboard.

11चिह्न · the sign

Stroke-based. Hand-drawn where it earns.

All UI icons are stroke 1.5, 24×24, rounded caps and joins. The hand-drawn pieces (wax seal, trail glyph, cabin window) are SVG-illustrated, not lifted from an icon set.

12रिक्त · the absent

What we did not do.

Restraint is the absence of the obvious move. Each refusal below was considered and removed.

  • Streak counters"12 nights in a row" is a SaaS mechanic. Stays are seasonal, not consecutive.
  • Membership tiers / badgesReplaced by Mudra (wax seals) — earned by being there, not by booking volume.
  • Push notifications during stayThe Companion never pings while the cabin is being used. Letters live in a feed.
  • Discount banners · "limited time"Saved screen carries no offer ribbon. Pricing is one screen deeper.
  • "Share to story" buttonsThe mudra is a private record, not Instagram content. Sharing is a personal introduction.
  • Star ratings · review countsPast trips show a quoted letter from the host, not a 5-star slot.
  • Skeleton loadersIf a screen needs to load, it shows ambient surface. The retreat does not wait for the spinner.
  • "Pair with…" upsellsThe kitchen suggests; it never sells. The footnote is enough: "tell Aarav if something isn't right."
  • Confirmation modals on destructive actionsSign out is one tap. Release-from-saved is one tap. The cabin will be there.

17पहुँच · the reach

Quiet, accessible, by default.

Restraint should never become illegibility. Contrast meets WCAG AA minimum; AAA for primary text. Tap targets ≥ 44 px. Focus rings visible. Motion respects prefers-reduced-motion.

Tap targets

44 × 44 px minimum, always. Bottom-tab icons, stepper buttons, and tappable rows are sized to this floor even when the rendered glyph is smaller.

Targets that look smaller (heart-save toggle on cards, list-row chevrons) extend their hit area with invisible padding.

Focus state

Keyboard focus is a 2 px bindu outline with a 2 px cream offset (a halo). Inputs use a clay bottom-border instead of an outer ring.

Mouse-only focus is suppressed via :focus-visible. The outline only appears on keyboard interaction.

Motion

Animations honour prefers-reduced-motion. The 6 s window-glow loop becomes a static gradient. Cross-fades become instant swaps.

No motion lasts longer than 700 ms. Nothing autoplays. Carousels do not exist.

Screen readers

Every actionable icon gets an aria-label. Devanagari decorative marks use aria-hidden="true" when paired with an English label.

Bottom-sheet open / close is announced via role="dialog" + focus trap.

19बिंदु · the point

The Bindu — one point of presence.

Search, voice, and the retreat companion in a single clay object. A still ceramic stone at rest; alive only on touch. The interaction nucleus of the app — not a floating button, not an assistant orb.

— The six states
RestA matte ceramic disc, magnetically held in thumb reach. Still — no glow, no breath. Presence is material, not motion.
TouchSettles inward under the finger (scale 0.94, deeper inner shade). A weighted, ceramic press — no ripple, no flash.
SearchThe search layer expands from the Bindu's corner; the clay becomes the field's leading mark. Editorial input, contextual suggestions, recents.
VoiceThe room quiets behind a scrim; a single calm ring ripples from the clay. Material, not a neon waveform. No assistant identity.
ProcessingA brief pause — three clay dots, one quiet breath. No spinner, no progress bar.
ResultsThe panel settles into short list rows — cabins, actions, or a route. One line each. Tap the dim to contract back into the Bindu.

Brand note · founder-approved The interaction Bindu carries an extremely subtle ambient breath (a 5.5s, microscopic rise) and a soft clay warmth while listening — approved as the living presence of the object. This is distinct from the sacred wordmark bindu, which remains still and never glows (BOS Ch.15 / Ch.40). The motion is meditative, never attention-seeking, and yields entirely to prefers-reduced-motion.
