— In context

A forest plate in a row of light icons.

The forest tile is dark by intent — it absorbs light from the home-screen wallpaper instead of competing with it. The full EKAM wordmark reads at touch size; below 60 px renders, the system falls back to the large monogram E+bindu. No glyph, no gradient, no shadow.

— Discipline

  • One stroke1.5 px for UI · 2 px for brand glyphs · 2.4 px for signage. Never thicker.
  • One accentBindu (#B4613A) is the only saturated colour. The dot itself is never animated.
  • Two materialsForest plates (outdoor signage) and cream paper (in-app, social). Nothing else.
  • Sizes lockUI 16/20/24 · brand glyphs 48/56/64 · signage 280×320 mm · cabin plates 200×140 mm.

— Never

  • Emoji, anywhere🌲 🏡 ☕ are banned. Use the Tier glyphs or the Lucide set.
  • Red-circle prohibitionThe clay diagonal does the same job, without the airport vocabulary.
  • Filled or chunky icon stylesNo Material-fill, no Phosphor-bold. Lucide stroke is the canon.
  • Animated app icon or wordmarkThe bindu does not pulse on the home screen. The wordmark is not a logo intro.
