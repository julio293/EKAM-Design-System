Bसंग्रह · the moodboard

Moodboard · the working palette.

Eleven references — eight from the original reference batch, three newly promoted. This is the register every working photograph is checked against.

Subject The cabin within the land — tea terraces, forest ridges, dusk skies. People only as silhouettes.
Frame Wide. The cabin is small inside a large landscape. Aerials used sparingly, never as the headline.
Light Dusk · pre-dawn · overcast. One warm window glow inside if at night. Never harsh midday.
Camera 35mm full-frame or medium format. Drone allowed for the land, never the cabin.
Grade Saturation −8 to −12%. Blacks lifted ≤ 5%. Whites slightly warmed. Greens preserved.
Mood Patient. Quiet. Lived-in. Earth-tone register; never cool blue resort cast.
