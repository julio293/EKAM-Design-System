— Do
  • Let the bindu breathe. Never below 6 px.
  • Use wide letterspacing. The calm comes from the space.
  • Deploy the bindu once per section as punctuation.
  • Use clay sparingly — one accent per composition.
— Don't
  • Replace the bindu with a flower, mark, or stylised dot.
  • Set the wordmark condensed, italic, or all-lowercase.
  • Scatter bindu dots around the layout for "decoration".
  • Flood layouts with clay. Signature becomes billboard.
