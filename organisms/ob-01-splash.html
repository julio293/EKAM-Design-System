Motion
splash-arrive: 1400ms ease-arrive. The wordmark block fades in from 12px below. Header and footer are static. The ambient ridge SVG is static. No looping or ambient animation on the splash screen. Reduced-motion: duration collapses to 0.001ms, no translateY.
Behaviour & routing
The entire dark surface is the tap target. No individual buttons are rendered. "Touch to begin" is the only textual affordance — it does not look like a button. Tapping anywhere advances to OB-02 Welcome Letter.

Returning users bypass splash entirely. It only runs on the first onboarding pass. Never shown twice to the same account.
Brand rule check
✓ No rounded corners (border-radius 42px belongs to the phone frame, not this organism)
✓ No exclamation marks in copy
✓ Bindu dot at cap-midline, never on baseline
✓ Sanskrit as a mark, not a label
✓ 60/30/8/2 colour ratio respected on dark surface
✓ No gradients on text
✓ Hard-edge doctrine — no internal rounded elements
