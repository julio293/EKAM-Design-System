— The proposition

One cabin.
One landscape.

A different model from luxury hospitality. Singular over plural, considered over abundant, slow over scaled.

  • SingularOne cabin per landscape. Never a cluster, never a phase II.
  • SereneOff-grid where possible. Solar, rainwater, composting. The cabin disappears into the forest.
  • WildThe land is the experience. We do not landscape, theme, or curate it.

08.Founder noteOnce · usually as the deck's emotional centre
एकम्EKAM
— Founder note — 14 February 2026

The decision to build EKAM was made on a long walk above Almora, in the third winter after the company I'd helped build was sold. The mountain was indifferent, which was the relief.

What followed was not a business plan. It was a question — what would hospitality look like if the discipline was restraint, not abundance? The answer is what we are building, one cabin at a time.

— Aarav · Co-founder

13.Operational framework3-up · process · stages · principles
एकम्EKAM
— Framework
— Operating model

How each cabin is run.

01. Resident host

One person lives on site. The host knows the trails, brews the chai, answers in under an hour, 7am – 9pm.

02. Local kitchen

Three-person kitchen cooks what the village is cooking. Satvik thalis on the day, breakfast on the house.

03. Land-led calendar

The cabin closes when the forest needs the season. No bookings in heavy monsoon or first snowfall. The cabin rests.
