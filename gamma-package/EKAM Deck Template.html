— Chapters IV & V · Retention

Mudra.
No tiers, no badges.

Loyalty is rebuilt entirely. A wax-sealed journal arrives in the post after each stay; the app mirrors that artifact. Referrals are a personal postcard, not a coupon.

  • Saved Escapes · empty stateAmbient map + three starter cabins. The first decision, not a paragraph about absence.
  • Mudra · the sealsVisited cabins as clay dots on a quiet HP/UK map. Unvisited as outlines. No progress bar.
  • Mudra balance1 mudra = ₹1. Applied to 20% of any booking. Earned by introductions + milestones.
  • Referral · invitationPostcard preview, Caveat note, ₹1,000 after their first stay.

08 / 09
— The design system

One language.
Six surfaces.

Eight colour tokens. Six typefaces. One easing curve. One accent (clay). Everything else is restraint.

  • ColoursCream / Sand / Bone / Mist / Moss / Forest / Ink + one accent (Bindu clay).
  • TypeCormorant Garamond display · Fraunces italic editorial · Raleway body · Inter UI · Tiro Devanagari · Caveat hand.
  • Motioncubic-bezier(0.22, 0.68, 0.16, 1) for everything except the cabin-window glow (6s natural loop).
  • ButtonsPill radius always. Clay primary · ghost outline · underlined link. WCAG 44px tap.
