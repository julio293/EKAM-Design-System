The Card.

The most-used surface in the product. One canonical implementation in components-canonical/Card.css — chapters consume it; they do not redefine it. Documented in EKAM Human Interface System.md §7.4.

01रचना · the anatomy

One photograph. One title. One question answered.

Every element earns its place. If a chapter needs to add to this anatomy, the answer is usually to remove something else.

View the cabin
  1. Photo slot. 4:5 by default; 16:11 for wide trip cards. Replace the striped placeholder with a real <img>. Never overlay the wordmark, a price tag, or a star rating on the photograph.
  2. Tier glyph. 40×33dp default. Identifies Kutir / Van / Shikhar / Chai at a glance. Required on every cabin card. See HIS §7.9.
  3. Eyebrow. Inter 500, 10px, 3px tracking, clay, uppercase. The chapter / sub-brand / region trail. EKAM · Kutir · Kalpa.
  4. Save heart. 32×32dp, cream square with bone hairline. aria-pressed toggles the clay fill. No backdrop-filter, no shadow.
  5. Title. Cormorant Garamond 400, 28px (md). Editorial. One sentence. "A cabin at 2,400m above the apple orchards." Not a SEO string.
  6. Body. Raleway 400, 14px, moss. One short line. Optional in --sm variant.
  7. Metadata. Inter 400, 11px. Bold for the data points; light for connectors. Separator is the brand mid-dot ·.
  8. CTA row. 18px clay rule + uppercase label + chevron. The whole card is tappable; the row is the visible affordance.
